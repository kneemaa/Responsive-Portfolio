About Me

I'm currently a Systems Adminstrator at Daqri, where we make wearable Augmented Reality Goggles and Helmets. My bread and butter is working in the startup world; I got my start at Hulu and progressed to Zefr and Katch before landing where I am now. I strive for system stability and automation, with aspirations to become a cloud administrator.
